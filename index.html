Know More

Delhi is one of India's major urban cities where 'marginalised communities' is a ubiquitous social category. SHINE is one such organisation which works WITH and FOR these communities with a simple motto - Self-Reliance!

Shine Foundation contributes towards development of marginalized urban slum communities of Delhi and NCR. A humble beginning with 70 children in an alternative education programmes, today Shine caters to more than 1000 direct beneficiaries through integrated community developments programmes.
